Reservoir and Source Rock Definition

Describe reservoir characteristics through integration of electric logs, surface and well bore seismic data, and core data. This would include reservoir structure, lithology, petrophysical properties, thermal maturity and rock mechanics.

Well Planning

From derived reservoir properties, define logging, coring, wellbore seismic and directional drilling plans. Also define horizontal landing point and azimuth of lateral. Pick drilling location based on "sweet spot" analysis.
